Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

An easy american chocolate chip cookie recipe, with a soft and chewy texture that is sure to be a hit with everyone!


    Ingredients (Makes 24):

  • 225g (8oz) unsalted butter
  • 350g (12.3oz) soft light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 400g (14oz) plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda (baking soda)
  • 225g (8oz) dark chocolate, chopped

      • Steps:

    1. Cream the butter and sugar together
    2. Add the eggs one at a time, mix well
    3. Beat in the vanilla extract
    4. Gently fold in the flour and bicarbonate of soda.
    5. Stir in the chocloate chunks and then chill the cookie dough in the fridge. (Try not to eat it.)
    6. Spoon balls of the cookie dough onto greased baking trays. Bake at 170c (325 F, Gas Mark 3) for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
