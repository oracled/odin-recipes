Gingerbread Men (Or women! Or people.)

Description

A festive classic that can still be enjoyed all year round, these easy to make biscuits are a treat for all the family. You can have them plain, or for a bit of extra fun, adorn them with icing and buttons!


    Ingredients (Makes 24):

  • 350g/12oz plain flour, plus extra for rolling out
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 125g/4Â½oz butter
  • 175g/6oz light soft brown sugar
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 4 tbsp golden syrup

      • Steps:

    1. Sift together the flour, bicarbonate of soda, ginger and cinnamon and pour into the bowl alongside the butter.
    2. Mix well with a whisk or blend with a food processor until the mixture looks like breadcrumbs.
    3. Stir in the sugar.
    4. Lightly beat the egg and golden syrup together in a separate bowl, then add to the other mixture.
    5. Mix/blend well until the mixture clumps together to form a dough, then knead until smooth.
    6. Wrap the dough in cling film and leave to chill in the fridge for 15 minutes
    7. Preheat the oven to 180/160c Fan/Gas 4, and then line two baking trays with greaseproof paper.
    8. Remove dough from fridge and roll out to a 0.5cm/1/4inch thickness on a lightly floured surface.
    9. Using a cutter, cut out the gingerbread men and place on the baking trays, making sure to leave gaps between them.
    10. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
    11. Remove from oven and leave on the tray for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool.
    12. When cooled feel free to decorate with icing and decorations (optional)
